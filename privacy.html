Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2023

Who We Are

This is the personal website of Don Barnetson, located at donbarnetson.com.

What Data We Collect

This is a static website with no user accounts, comments, or tracking cookies. The site does not use analytics services or tracking scripts.

Contact Form

If you submit the contact form, your name, email address, and message are sent to Formspree for processing. Formspree's privacy policy governs the handling of this data. We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry.

Embedded Content

Articles on this site may include embedded content (e.g. videos, images, articles). Embedded content from other websites behaves in the same way as if the visitor has visited that website directly. These websites may collect data about you, use cookies, embed additional third-party tracking, and monitor your interaction with the embedded content.

Fonts

This site loads the Inter font from Google Fonts. Google may collect your IP address and browser information as part of this request. See Google's Privacy Policy for more information. Monospace fonts (IBM Plex Mono) are self-hosted and involve no third-party requests.

Hosting

This site is hosted as a static website. The hosting provider may collect standard server logs including IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited. These logs are used for operational purposes only and are not shared with third parties.

Your Rights

If you have submitted information via the contact form, you may request that we delete your data by reaching out through the same form or via LinkedIn.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
